CAULIFLOWER SABJI

Ingredients:

Cauliflower(gobi)- 2 cups, cut into small florets

Potato- 1 medium, diced

Oil- 2 tbsp

Cumin seeds- 1/2 tsp

Green chilli- 1, finely chopped

Onion- 1 medium, finely chopped

Tomato- 1 medium, finely chopped

Ginger-garlic paste- 1/2 tsp

Turmeric powder- 1/4 tsp

Red chilli powder- 1/2tsp(adjust according to taste)

Coriander powder- 1 tsp

Garam masala- 1/4 tsp

Salt- to taste

Fresh coriander leaves- for garnish

Procedure

Step 1: Prep the cauliflower
Wash the florets an dsoak them in hot water with a pinch of salt.
Drain well.

Step 2: Tempering
Heat oil in a pan
Add cumin seeds, let them crackle.
Add green chilli and chopped onion, saute until light golden.

Step 3: Add base flavors
Add ginger garlic paste, saute for 30 seconds
add chopped tomato, cook until soft.
Add turmeric powder, red chilli powder, coriander powder and salt.
Mix well.

Step 4: Cook vegetables
Add cauliflower and potato, stir to coat with masala.
Sprinkle 2-3 tbsp of water, cover and cook on low flame until tender(abou 10-15 minutes) stirring occaisonally.

Step 5: Finish
Once cooked sprinkle garam masala, mix and garnish with fresh coriander leaves

Serving ideas:
Best enjoyed hot with roti, paratha or dal-rice.
